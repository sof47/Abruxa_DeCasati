About Me

Hi, I’m Sofía Ballesteros, better known as Abruxa DeCasati a creative and multidisciplinary artist with expertise in digital and traditional art, 3D modeling, 2D animation, web design, and video game development. With a background from the Universidad Iberoamericana (Ibero) in Mexico, I’ve spent years cultivating a passion for crafting immersive, visually compelling experiences across various platforms. My work blends a rich understanding of design principles with technical expertise, allowing me to tackle everything from high-quality 3D visuals and animations to interactive web projects.

Whether working on video games or interactive installations, I focus on creating art that is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and engaging. I’ve had the privilege of collaborating with incredible clients such as La Casa Azul: Museo de Frida Kahlo, Museo del Virreinato Tepozotlán, and Huerto Ibero, where I brought my artistic vision to life through creative direction and cutting-edge design. Each project has further refined my skills and deepened my passion for the intersection of art and technology. If you're looking for a creative partner to bring your ideas to life through innovative design and artistic storytelling, let's work together to turn your vision into reality.
