Seedless Demo

Seedles (Demo) Seedles is a demo for my original video game of the same name, created entirely by me using Unity. The game tells the story of two boys who are transformed into lemons by a mischievous witch. To return to their human forms, they must navigate a series of challenges and escape the witch's clutches. The demo features the Kitchen Level, where players explore a whimsical yet perilous environment filled with puzzles, obstacles, and witchy surprises. This level showcases the core mechanics, art style, and narrative tone of the game, offering a glimpse into the full adventure. As a solo project, I handled all aspects of development, including programming, level design, art, and sound.
